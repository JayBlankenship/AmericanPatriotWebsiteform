Benefits of Attending the Fair

Personalized Counseling

You will have opportunity for one-one-one meeting with counselors and University representatives at the Fairs

Free Admission Processing

You will get your offer to your desired university at zero cost

Access to Funding & Scholarships

You will obtain useful information on funding and partial scholarship opportunities

Instant Admissions

You will get instant offers into the participating Universities

Post Study Work Opportunities

You will get free information on 2 Years post study work opportunities in a number of countries

Visa Support

You will get free visa counseling, support and guidance

English Test Discount

You will have opportunity to take English test at a discount rate and also access free preparatory materials

Post Departure Support

Participants will have access to post departure pastoral support abroad

Admission Interview Support

You will receive guidance on how to prepare your personal statements and how to respond to admission interviews.
